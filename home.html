Orthopaedic Research Society Annual Meeting 2026

Dr. Andreassen will be presenting work from the Mayo Clinic on Statistical Shape and Intensity Models of the Human Wrist in the NIRA Medical Imaging Session on Saturday March, 28 at 4:45 - 5:00 pm, and as a NIRA poster in the poster sessions, Poster#61. This work combines novel techniques using publicly-available non-linear morphing algorithms with photon counting detector CT images to develop a complete shape, intensity, and recently pose, model of the full human wrist joint.
