Public Datasets

Dr. Andreassen is committed to open science and reproducible research. He has provided significant contributions to the scientific community by making his research models, data, and software openly available:

  • Computational models, data, and software for personalized wrist modeling
  • FEMORS Toolbox for non-linear mesh morphing and overclosure removal
  • Full 3D geometries and segmentations of Visible Human Male and Female
  • Validated specimen-specific finite element models of the knee
  • Data, models, and documentation for knee modeling from KneeHub Project
  • Personalized musculoskeletal models of the shoulder with total shoulder arthroplasty

These open contributions enable other researchers to validate findings, extend methodologies, and advance the field of computational biomechanics.
